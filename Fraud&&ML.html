Transaction monitoring

Here in CZRemit we monitor all transactions for suspicious activity. This includes transactions that are unusually large , suspicous and frequent. If our staff or systems identifies any suspicous activity we will hold or even cancel the transaction and will immediately report it to the relevant authorities immdiately such as Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS).

Customer identification and verification

We require customers to to provide personal information such as their name, address, date of birth, and government-issued ID. By doing so it will help prove your identity to us. We will then send a 6 digit OTP code to your registered mobile number to ensure that you are the righful owner logging into your precious account.This is a form of 2FA to ensure you are the right person that is logging in and it will greatly reduced the risk unauthorized attempts to access your accounts.

Customer Due Dilligence

Here at CZRemit we conduct customer due dilligence on all customers be it how big how important each customer are. Such as understanding what how much money customers want to sent and what are the origins of their funds. These will help us at CZRemit combat money laundering.
